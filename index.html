Questions

Is this legal advice?

Counterdraft is a contract-analysis tool, not a law firm, and doesn't replace an attorney for high-stakes deals. It gives you a professional-grade starting point and the leverage to negotiate.

What contracts does it handle?

Common US commercial agreements and Ukrainian commercial contracts: vendor/supplier, leases, MSAs, NDAs, services and contractor deals.

Is my contract data safe?

Yes. Documents are encrypted in transit, processed for your audit only, and never used to train public models or stored long-term.

How accurate is it?

The risk logic is designed by a practicing contracts attorney and refined continuously. Every flag includes the reasoning so you can judge it yourself.
